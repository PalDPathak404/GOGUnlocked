⚠️ ⚠️ RULES FOR REQUESTING UPDATES ⚠️ ⚠️

Before sending a game request check the following: ⬇️

A. The latest update must be released and on GOG.com. We don't update games based on steam updates, only GOG.

B. Keep your update requests 12 hours apart. Any spam will automatically be deleted.

OUR MISSION

We believe in DRM-FREE gaming. That's why we launched GOG Unlocked so you (as a user) can test out a game before deciding on purchasing it! Developers work incredibly hard to create amazing PC games just for you. If you love a game, we highly encourage you to purchase it on the official store.
